Rosé
Park

Rosé, born in New Zealand in 1997, is a singer, songwriter, and dancer. She joined BLACKPINK in 2016, a highly successful K-pop group. Known for her unique voice and guitar skills, she debuted as a solo artist with "On The Ground" in 2021. She's a beloved figure in the entertainment industry.
