Jennie, born in South Korea in 1996, is a singer, rapper, and dancer. She joined BLACKPINK in 2016, a highly successful K-pop group. Known for her powerful rap verses and stage presence, she's a prominent figure in the music industry. Jennie has released solo tracks like "SOLO" and "Gone," showcasing her versatility as an artist.
